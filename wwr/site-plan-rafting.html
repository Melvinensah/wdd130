Overview

Purpose

Our purpose at White Water is to invite people to feel and experience the beauty and relaxation
that is afforded us by the beautiful rivers.That's why we subscribe to the Dry Oar philosophy of boating.
keeping your oars dry for us means taking time to look around and notice the beauty that surrounds
the rivers we love.The river will not only provide relaxation, but also a thrill of a life time.
We, at Dry Oar, invite you to experience the rapids for an exhilarating thrill which is followed by calm
beautiful waters.The river experience and the surrounding views will awe you and leave you with wanting more.
Join us again,and again for both an invigorating and relaxing experience.

Audience

Our audience are mainly those that are visiting the lake Sonfon in the north end of sierra leone and are interested
in having fun at the river rapids. Though we target those that are aged 18 and older, an individual aged 16 can join with
others who are older in the same boat.Tourist are aminly our best target and giving them the quality time they deserve has
been our priority. Others who aren't tourist but that are interested are also invited to feel the relaxation and beauty of the
river provided they can pay for the beautiful experience.
